Uses

Personal

Some robots may perform only a limited number of tasks, but the Ridge Robot also pertains to your lifestyle at home. It can be told to clean(vacuum, lawn mowing, window clean, etc.), provide domestic security and surveillance, give daily reminders, and even take care for your pets.

Educational

In the educational field, our Ridge Robot assists both the teacher and the student. For the teachers, it can automate activities such as grading, provide input in addition to a teacher's remarks/lessons, and even act as a tutor for fundamentals of math and writing. As for the students in the classroom, our robot can learn adaptatively and respond to various individual student's needs, whether it be their pace, technique, or effort. In addition, it is able to point out to students where they should improve and focus on, serve as a subsitute teacher or tutor, and facilitate suggestions as to what classes students should take and what preparation may be necessary to ease the transitions made in high school or college.

Recreational

In addition to its practical uses, the robot can help in a variety of recreational activities. Individually, people can use the robot to quickly book flight or concert tickets, practice sports through its unique "rebounding" ability for basketball, baseball, soccer, and many more, or even act as an interactive toy for all ages. On a larger scale, the robot can run applications that will allow professional sports leaders understand emerging trends within their respective industrieS, automate highlights and scores, and provide real-time footage.

Commercial/Business

Already an established use within enterprise, our robot can ddo more than the modern business and commercial intelligence AI robots. The Ridge Robot, in addition to identifying trends, suggesting insights and decisions based upon data, predicting repairs and managing machinery and manufacturing, it is also able to handle management level requests, work effectively when taking on the tasks of many jobs, and prevent fraud and identity theft through its protective control.

Future Developments

The motivated individuals at Ridge TSA are consistently working diligently to create even more functions and applications to either future models or the Ridge Robot. Current projects include broader communication/messaging through a third-party application, adaptative techniques that enable schools to identify at-risk students to decrease dropout rates, provide sound advice in the investing field and monitor the stock market trends as displayed through modern APIs, and lastly partner with commercial stores and online retailers to automate shopping processes for necessary missing items and the packages' safe arrivals.
